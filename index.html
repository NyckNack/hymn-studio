The Process

From Vision to Streaming

1

Discovery Call

A quick conversation to understand your church, your vision, and whether we're a good fit. We'll schedule a weekday that works for your worship pastor — never Wednesdays or Sundays. We're in church those days too.

2

Day One — Culture & Sound

We fly to your church and sit down with your worship pastor (and front-of-house engineer, if available). We dive into the culture of your church body, the styles you love, and listen to real-time examples together. Then we record a clean, high-quality vocal of your worship pastor.

3

Day Two — Direction & Departure

We present initial ideas based on everything from Day One. Once we're aligned on direction, we head home and get to work.

4

Production & Mastering

We hire professional studio musicians, mix and produce your music, then send it to a world-renowned mastering engineer. We make sure your church is completely happy with the final product before anything goes live.

5

Delivered to the World

Your finished music is uploaded to Spotify, Apple Music, and all major streaming platforms. From there, it's completely up to your church — share it, worship to it, use it to grow your community.
